  • Use a real computer mouse or a picture of one as reference.

  • Render it in Blender with Ray Tracing (realistic).
  • Document: Show us your work!

  • The goal is to get acquainted with FreeCAD modeling and Blender rendering.
  • It's best to start with a pencil sketch on paper, and then transfer the idea to digital form.
